Faq's

1. Can I see which colleagues are present in the office?
No, but you can see the percent of your colleagues who have reserved their desks at the same workplace as you.

2. I don't have a company floorplan, can I still use reserveyourdesk?
Yes, you can even try creating your own floorplans on reserveyourdesk!

3. I have a small business, is reserveyourdesk right for me?
Yes. Reserveyourdesk is supportive of B2B transactions! You can try to rent a workplace for your meetings,seminars or other gatherings. You can also try renting your place out when it's less occupied.

You can contact us for any other queries.
