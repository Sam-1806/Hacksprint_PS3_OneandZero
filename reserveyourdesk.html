Reserve Desk And Keep A Hold On Office
Occupancy At Your Fingertips.


With ReserveYourDesk companiescan easily create
workplaces and meeting rooms. They can keep a tight grip on
the occupancy and utilization rate. On the other hand
the employees can easily reserve desks at their workplaces
and meeting rooms.
ReserveYourDesk makes it
easier to find the ideal balance between working from home
and working at the office. But makes it simple
and without any hassle.















Flexible Offices

A combination of office work and home working is
the future of hybrid work, hence there is the need
of flexible offices.
















Get Started

Create an account. Upload the office floor plan and occupy
it with work and meeting places. Use timeslots
to indicate when which places can be reserved. And
have your users start using it!

















Workplace Management

Make specific spots available based on facilities,
or per user group. You can immediately see which places have
been reserved and when they will become available again.
And you have a helpful overview of whether your colleagues are present and where they are!




















Setting Up!

Which desks can you work at? Which meeting rooms
are in use, how many people are allowed
in the office at any given time? Find out by
getting your company to sign up!
